Website Redesign

Date: 2019
Services: paint.net/photoshop

Figure 1: Final Product





This project required Website Design and use of Photoshop by redesigning a website with poor deisgn elements.

Figure 2: Original Website


Figure 2 shows the website in its original state. The website was cluttered, had no search bar and no spacing which made navigation extremely difficult which would subsequently dismay customers and ruin initial impressions of the website.

Figure 1 shows the final product which takes into consideration spacing between content and important navigation options which helps customers navigate the site. The use of the dark theme is also prevelant in modern web design as it reduces strain on the eyes when reading content.
